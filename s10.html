About the Author

James Leonard grew up loving the wide open spaces of Arizona. A lover of western genre fiction, his passion is to write stories that celebrate life in the old west with heroes that uphold traditional values of moral, with pure motivations and who treats women and children well. James is the author of Western Justice series. His stories often set in realistic settings that honor the spirit of the old west with traits of perseverance, justice and loyalty. .
