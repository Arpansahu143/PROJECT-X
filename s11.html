About the Author

Ernesto A. Maisuls, M.D., born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, brings a wealth of experience and expertise to his role as a writer. As a novelist, he draws on his diverse background to craft engaging and thought-provoking narratives. His works reflect a profound understanding of human nature, intricate storytelling, and a remarkable ability to explore complex themes with finesse. Among his most notable works are "The Alien Artifact Series", "The Bioweapon", "The Seer's Portal: Short Stories II", "The Mandel's Tale", "The Perfect Human Imperfection", "Bina the Linker", and more.
