About the Author

Jacob Peppers lives in Armuchee, Georgia with his wife, three children, and three dogs. He is the author of multiple bestselling epic fantasy series including The Last Eternal, The Seven Virtues, and Saga of the Known Lands. His short fiction story, "The Lies of Autumn" was a finalist entry for the 2013 Eric Hoffer award for short prose.
