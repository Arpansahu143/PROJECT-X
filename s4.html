About the Author

Vanessa Zamarripa is a children's book author and a Hall of Fame inductee at UCLA and Region 5, recognized for her success in gymnastics. She is one of the most accomplished gymnasts in UCLA Bruins history and a former U.S. National Team member. In addition to her Hall of Fame honors, some of her most notable achievements during her tenure at UCLA include being the NCAA Vault Champion and a member of the UCLA Bruins women's gymnastics team that won the NCAA National Championship title. She earned nine perfect 10s on vault in her NCAA career and holds 19 All-American honors. During her senior season, she was named the NACGC/W Division I National Gymnast of the Year and received the AAI Award, given to the nation's top collegiate senior gymnast. In 2015, she was named to the Pac-12 All-Century Team, an honor recognizing the top gymnasts in the conference's 100-year history. After graduating with honors from UCLA, Vanessa pursued a career as a stuntwoman in the film industry, where she has worked for over ten years and has also expanded her career into the world of real estate
